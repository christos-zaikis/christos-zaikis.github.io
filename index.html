Brief Biography

I am a first-year PhD Candidate in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of School of Engineering of the University of Thessaly, Greece, advised by Professor Michael Vasilakopoulos My current research focuses on Large Language Models (LLMs) as well as their applications in recommender systems (RecSys).

I obtained my BSc degree in Computing Science from University of Western Macedonia ,Greece and My MSc from the Hellenic Open University.
